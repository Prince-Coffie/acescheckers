about us

Aces Checkers Consult Limited is a full-service business advisory and consultancy firm located at Dansoman-Accra with expertise in Book keeping, Auditing,Payroll & Invetory Management, Tax and Advisory services. Our aim is to assist our clients in efficiently managing their business transactions, filing their taxes, and maximizing their profits margins. At Aces, we take a client-focused approach and are always looking for ways to enhance the value we bring to our clients. We are determined to succeed and thrive on challenges, drawing inspiration from our clients' success. Our philosophy centers around delivering value-adding solutions and cost-effective services that help our clients achieve their financial goals.

frequently asked question

How much do you charge?

Our services are competitive and the cost will vary from business to business.Our rates are currently the lowest in the market and we provide sign-on discounts for new clients and we also have discounts for our cherished existing clients.

How is the monthly accounting procedure carried out?

Your source documents are the first step in our monthly accounting process. Our staff compiles the data and provides you with the following reports to assist you in regaining financial control:

  • Prepare monthly balance sheets
  • Prepare monthly profit and loss statements
  • Provide guidance based on your company's situation, tax planning, and financial statements.

What type of bookkeeping do you do?

Our services are typically performed more frequently, like once a week or daily. We handle the day-to-day bookkeeping operations of your business. We work for you or with you, running payroll and paying bills on a schedule of your choosing

Why should I choose Aces Checkers Consult?

The services provided ensure strict confidentiality and offer valuable information to effectively manage company or personal finances, leading to strategic thinking and efficient use of resources.
